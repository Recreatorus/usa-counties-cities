Briefly about US cities

As of 2019, there were 3143 counties (3,243 counties and county-equivalents) and about 30,000 cities in the United States. Of which: 4 cities, population of which there are more than 2 million people, 10 are millionaire cities (over 1 million people), 317 large cities with a population of over 100,000 people, 56 - large cities (from 300 thousand people to 1 million people) and 251 - large cities (from 100 to 300 thousand people). The United States 2020 population is estimated at 331,002,651 people at mid year according to UN data, urban population - 273,975,139 (82.8%), Median Age - 38.3.
